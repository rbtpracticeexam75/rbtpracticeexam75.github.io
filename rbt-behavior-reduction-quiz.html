RBT Behavior Reduction Quiz

Click an answer to see if you're correct and view the explanation!

1. What is the first step in addressing challenging behavior?
Functional assessments identify the cause of challenging behavior, guiding the development of effective interventions.
2. Which of the following is an example of extinction?
Extinction involves no longer providing reinforcement for a behavior, which should reduce its occurrence over time.
3. Differential reinforcement of alternative behavior (DRA) means:
DRA teaches the client an appropriate alternative while decreasing the undesired behavior.
4. Which data should an RBT collect for behavior reduction plans?
Structured data helps analyze patterns and evaluate the effectiveness of behavior reduction strategies.
5. Which of the following is NOT an ethical behavior reduction strategy?
Corporal punishment is unethical and not allowed under RBT or BACB guidelines.
