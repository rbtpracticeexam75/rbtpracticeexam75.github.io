RBT Assessment Quiz

Click an answer to see if you're correct and view the explanation!

1. What is the main purpose of a functional assessment in ABA?
Functional assessments help determine why a behavior occurs, which is critical for designing effective interventions.
2. Which type of data is most often used during assessment?
Objective, measurable data such as frequency, duration, and intensity is essential for accurate assessment and treatment planning.
3. Continuous measurement is best described as:
Continuous measurement captures all instances of the behavior, giving accurate data for analysis.
4. An RBT’s role in assessments includes:
RBTs collect data and implement assessments according to BCBA instructions, but do not independently design plans or make diagnoses.
5. What is inter-observer agreement (IOA)?
IOA ensures that data collection is reliable by comparing results from multiple observers.
